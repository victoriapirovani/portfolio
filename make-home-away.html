Make Home Away

The following is a limited edition Travel Accessories capsule collection for Argentina’s leading Manchester & Homewares retailer. With over 40 years in the industry, 72 stores across Argentina and an international e-commerce store, Arredo is a family owned company with a mission to supply every costumer with one of a kind products that strongly connect to their personal style.
Arredo is the destination for home fashion, with furniture, decoration, soft furnishings and everything for the bedroom, living room, bathroom and kitchen. Their multi-target strategy is one of the competitive advantages they owe their success to: catering to female, teenager and children audiences while also supplying integral manchester solutions for the hospitality industry. Loyal customers choose Arredo because of their wide range of great designs, manufactured with premium quality materials at low prices.
The challenge is to understand and satisfy consumers’ needs and expectations in a rapidly evolving market while expanding the offer on product categories and constantly innovating to deliver engaging proposals aligned with the latest global trends in home fashion.
Your home follows you wherever you go. #MAKEHOMEAWAY
Under the concept of #MakeHome, Arredo suggests we redefine our homes as shelters from everyday life and sanctuaries of recharge, by appealing to the importance of day to day moments such as preparing a meal with the family, enjoying a book by the window, playing with your children or simply rediscovering inner peace in your favourite corner - in that way restructuring our homes as refuges of true spiritual enrichment.
Inspired by that philosophy of making your place your home, this collection proposes an alternative of #MAKEHOME for those who frequently find themselves traveling away from theirs with a limited edition line of travel accessories. #MAKEHOMEAWAY focuses on bringing home closer to us by incorporating subtle comforting details into each design and a strong presence of print and pattern characteristic to the brand’s identity, while maintaining a playful idea of nostalgia across every aspect of the product.
