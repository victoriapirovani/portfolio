Secret Gardens

The following is part of a comprehensive Interior Design project aiming to transform a selection of hand painted floral illustrations into a collection of patterns - both wallpapers and textile pieces - that furnish the inside of a home.
A trip down memory lane featuring the imaginary gardens of a childhood home and the endless universe of wonder embedded within its walls.
Influenced by the works of William Morris and his contributions to the fields of design and architecture, this collection of prints serves as a portal to the past: it explores a childâ€™s memories of the artistic sensibility and intricateness in the wallpapers that decorated a mellow and sheltered childhood. Their poignant symmetry is an expression of the unwithering innocence in youth, whereas the illusion of depth by layering playfully points to the childish fantasy of wishing to experience the magical landscapes that unfold within the walls.
