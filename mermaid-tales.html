Mermaid Tales

The following is a collection of knit textiles for Fashion Design aimed to specifically respond to Balmain’s aesthetic and brand image; developed using industrial and experimental knitting technologies.
Mermaid tales about mermaid tails dancing in the glowing reef of lights.
Inspired by a fantasy about nightlife in the mermaids’ reef, the different sea depths are represented as a downwards transition from shimmering pastel colours and the subtle surface glow towards the iridescent darkness of the sea floor - where bioluminescence plays a key role. The above mentioned translates into textile designs through the juxtaposition of organic and seemingly unorganized volumes together with rather controlled curved structures, evoking the cyclical movement of waves as well as the morphology of marine life.
